Frequently Asked Questions / 常見問題

Q: 訂閱後如何恢復購買？
A: 開啟 App，前往「設定」→「恢復購買」即可還原您的訂閱內容。

Q: How do I restore my subscription?
A: Open the app, go to Settings → Restore Purchases.

Q: 如何取消訂閱？
A: 前往 iPhone「設定」→ Apple ID →「訂閱項目」，即可管理或取消 SWExam Pro 的訂閱。

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions, then manage or cancel SWExam Pro.

Q: 題庫更新頻率為何？
A: 題庫定期更新，涵蓋社會工作師國家考試最新歷屆試題。

Q: How often is the question bank updated?
A: The question bank is updated regularly to include the latest past exam questions for the Taiwan Social Worker National Exam.
