Privacy Policy (English)

Overview

PHExamPro ("the App") is an iOS application designed to help public health licensing exam candidates students prepare for the Taiwan national licensing examination. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data We Collect

Local Only (never transmitted): Study records, learning progress, weak point analysis, app preferences, question mastery data — stored via SwiftData on your device only.

Server Download (read-only): Question bank data downloaded from Supabase. No personal information is uploaded.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect your name, email, location, or any personally identifiable information. We do not use advertising SDKs, third-party analytics, or device identifiers.

3. Third-Party Services

Supabase: Hosts the question bank database. Only question data passes through Supabase. See supabase.com/privacy.

Apple StoreKit: Subscription purchases processed by Apple. We receive only purchase status confirmation.

4. Data Security

Question explanations are encrypted with AES-256-GCM. All server communication uses HTTPS/TLS. Local data is protected by iOS built-in data protection.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is designed for users aged 17 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Data Deletion

To delete all local data: Settings → Reset All Records, or simply uninstall the App.

7. Contact

📧 myhsieh@me.com

隱私權政策（繁體中文）

概述

PHExamPro 是協助公共衛生師學生備考台灣公共衛生師國家考試的 iOS 應用程式，由獨立開發者開發與維護。我們重視您的隱私。

1. 收集與儲存的資料

僅儲存於裝置（不傳送）：學習紀錄、學習進度、弱點分析、偏好設定、題目熟練度。

從伺服器下載（唯讀）：從 Supabase 下載題庫資料，不會上傳任何個人資料。

2. 我們不收集的資料

我們不收集您的姓名、電子郵件、位置，或任何可識別個人身分的資訊。不使用廣告 SDK 或第三方分析工具。

3. 第三方服務

Supabase：託管題庫資料庫。隱私政策：supabase.com/privacy

Apple StoreKit：訂閱購買由 Apple 處理，我們僅取得購買狀態確認。

4. 資料安全

題目解說以 AES-256-GCM 加密，所有通訊使用 HTTPS/TLS，本機資料受 iOS 資料保護機制保護。

5. 兒童隱私

本 App 專為 17 歲以上用戶設計，不針對 13 歲以下兒童。

6. 資料刪除

設定頁面 → 重置所有學習紀錄，或直接解除安裝本 App。

7. 聯絡方式

📧 myhsieh@me.com
