Privacy Policy (English)

Overview

SWExam Pro ("the App") is an iOS application designed to help candidates prepare for the Taiwan Social Worker National Licensing Examination. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data We Collect

Local Only (never transmitted): Exam practice records, learning progress, app preferences, and question mastery data — stored locally on your device only.

Subscription Status: Purchase confirmation received from Apple StoreKit. No personal information is shared with us.

Server Download (read-only): Question bank data downloaded from Supabase. No personal information is uploaded.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect your name, email, location, or any personally identifiable information. We do not use advertising SDKs, third-party analytics, or device identifiers.

3. Third-Party Services

Supabase: Hosts the question bank database. Only question data passes through Supabase. See supabase.com/privacy.

Apple StoreKit: Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We receive only a purchase status confirmation — no payment details are shared with us.

4. Data Security

Question explanations are encrypted. All server communication uses HTTPS/TLS. Local data is protected by iOS built-in data protection.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is designed for adult exam candidates. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Data Deletion

To delete all local data: Settings → Reset All Records, or simply uninstall the App.

7. Contact

隱私權政策（繁體中文）

概述

SWExam Pro（以下簡稱「本 App」）是專為社會工作師國家考試考生設計的 iOS 應用程式。我們致力於保護您的隱私。

1. 我們收集的資料

僅儲存於本機（不對外傳送）：考試練習紀錄、學習進度、App 偏好設定及題目熟練度資料——儲存於您的裝置本機。

訂閱狀態：透過 Apple StoreKit 收到購買確認通知，我們不會取得任何個人資料。

從伺服器下載（唯讀）：題庫資料從 Supabase 下載。不上傳任何個人資訊。

2. 我們不收集的資料

我們不收集您的姓名、電子郵件、位置或任何可識別個人身分的資訊。我們不使用廣告 SDK、第三方分析工具或裝置識別碼。

3. 第三方服務

Supabase：托管題庫資料庫，僅題目資料通過 Supabase。請參閱 supabase.com/privacy。

Apple StoreKit：訂閱購買完全由 Apple 處理。我們僅收到購買狀態確認，不取得任何付款細節。

4. 資料安全

題目解析內容已加密處理。所有伺服器通訊使用 HTTPS/TLS。本機資料受 iOS 內建資料保護機制保護。

5. 兒童隱私

本 App 適合成年考生使用。我們不故意收集 13 歲以下兒童的資訊。

6. 刪除資料

如需刪除所有本機資料：設定 → 重置所有紀錄，或直接解除安裝本 App。

7. 聯絡方式

📧 myhsieh@me.com
